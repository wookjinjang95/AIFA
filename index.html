About AIFA

AIFA (Artificial Intelligence for All) is a software company that believes A.I world. A world which is coming faster than we expected. THe main business goal of AIFA is to provide easy and cheap access of A.I tools for everyone. To prove that, certain things need to be confirmed.

Insights for Everyone

Currently, many big corporations or industries such as Facebook, Google, and Amazon are moving towards artificial intelligence, short for A.I. A.I is a tool that allows machines or computers to obtain massive amount of data and perform analysis or engineers call it, predictive analysis, to predict the future. As said, A.I is not only developed to replicate neural networks of our brain's process but also to predict stock market by using historical data from previous stock market behaviors. Google is also a great example that demonstrates the potentials of A.I.
