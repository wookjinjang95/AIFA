Our Story

AIFA stands for Artificial Intelligence for All. We believe that any advaned tools such as A.I algorithms
or statistics can be used in our daily lives. Currently, we have big corporations such as Google, Amazon
and Facebook using these tools to maximize their profit and advertising. However, AIFA feels unfair that
ordinay people don't have access to these advanced tools to benefit our daily lives and decision making.
By allowing these advanced tools to be easily accessable for everyone, AIFA believes that it brings equality
for both people and corporations.

Why Vehicle Industry?

As a lead of AIFA, I personally felt that consumers are having hard time purchasing vehicles. To consumers,
a vehicle can be secondary home for a family, or a product that brings joy and excitement. However, to
to purhcase a single car is difficult. Many platforms like Carfax and CarWoW tells consumers if the price
is above average or below average pricing. But we want to know more. This is where data
analysis will come and help.
